Act I:

The party crashed on Xen'drik in an airship and, with Luc’s guidance, made their way to Stormreach, helping invaders seize the city using a khyber shard. Their journey led them to Sharn, where Luc revealed his membership in the Aurum and his true allegiance to the Dreaming Dark. He manipulated the group to bring back a wizard’s apprentice, using him to open a portal that caused the catastrophic fall of Sharn’s Upper City. In the chaos, the party battled Luc and the halfling Eberron James, but both antagonists escaped.

Act II:

Fleeing the destruction of Sharn in an airship, the group fought a dramatic aerial battle before encountering the Lords of Dust. They escaped through a dream portal to Fairhaven, where they faced Luc’s minions and nightmare creatures tailored to each character’s fears. Eberron James reappeared to assist them. The act concluded with the revelation that Luc had escaped to Sarlona, beginning a ritual to allow the Dreaming Dark to fully invade Eberron and usher in an age of darkness.
