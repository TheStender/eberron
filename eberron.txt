Eberron Act I summary:

After crashing on Xen'drik in an airship, the party worked with a man named Luc to make their way to Stormreach. They found the city under attack, but were able to sneak in. They used a khyber shard they had found in a temple to help the invaders take the city. 

They made their way to Sharn where Luc revealed he was a member of the Aurum, and he wanted their help to turn Breland against its neighbors and start a war. The players made their way through the continent searching for a master wizard, but found he had been killed. Instead they brought his apprentice back to Sharn.

Luc then revealed he was part of the Dreaming Dark, and he was using the apprentice to open a portal to allow the Dreaming Dark to enter the world to destroy it. This entailed crashing the Upper City of Sharn down into the city, causing tens of thousands of deaths. The players battled Luc, all except for a halfling named Eberron James, who fought the party. Both Luc and Eberron escaped.
